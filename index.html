Computer Networks

Before Computer Networks, people had to send letters between each other and that was really time consuming. It took 2 to 3 weeks to send and receive information and sometimes messages were never delivered.

Now we have information being transferred at the speed of light(Latency:the time it takes for a message to transfer) on fibre optic cables across the globe

The concept of Computer Networks are hugely dependent on two terms: LAN(Local Area Network) and WAN(Wide Area Network)
